Chicken Soup

Description

A big bowl of chicken noodle soup is always hot, refreshing, cold-repellinig and healthy.

Homemade chicken noodle soup is the best.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole roasted chicken
  • 4 large carrots
  • 4 stalks of celery
  • 2 inch handful of linguini
  • chicken boullion

Steps

  1. Unique first step - Eat about half of the chicken because its too much for this recipe.
  2. Place uneaten chicken and carcas into two gallon pot with one gallon of water.
  3. Cook on low to medium until meat begins to fall from the bone.
  4. Let chicken and broth cool, then seperate meat from bone, cartilage and other waste.
    • for extra flavor puree skin and add to broth.
  6. Add broth, chopped carrots and chopped celery into empty pot.
  7. Cook on medium for about 15 minutes
  8. Add more water and chicken boullion as needed for flavor.
  9. Add linguini noodles and continue cooking on low until noodles are soft.
  10. Add salt and pepper to tase.
  11. Serve in large bowls
  12. Enjoy!!
