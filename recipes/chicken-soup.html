Chicken Soup

Description

This flavorful recipe has been cooked in the same style for generations. It is inexpensive, feeds many people and is great for preventing a hangover.

While menudo can be made in different ways, this particular version is clear or blanco.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1 pound tripe
  • 2 pounds uncooked hominy (nixtamal)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 teaspon oregano
  • garlic
  • salt

Extra Toppings Optional

  • lime
  • crushed peppers, chili tepin for more heat
  • green onions
  • jalapenos
  • cabbage
  • radishes

Steps

  1. Fill three gallon pot with two gallons of water.
  2. Thouroughly clean then cut tripe into bite-size pieces and place into the pot. Traditionally cut into large bites. However, can be more palatable in smaller bites.
  3. Place hominy into a strainer, rinse thoroughly and dump contents into the pot.
  4. Place whole, peeled onion into pot.
  5. Add oregano.
  6. Add garlic to own preference.
  7. Heat pot to boil, turn down heat to simmer for approximately 3 hours.
  8. Add salt to own preference after hominy has popped, nearly doubles in size.
  9. Serve in bowls and add extra toppings as desired.
  10. Enjoy!!
