Chili Beans

Description

This healthy and satisfying recipe is a filling as it is beautiful.

Chili is a common favorite because of its simplicity, cheap ingredients and heartiness.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups dried pinto beans
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 tablespoon garlic
  • salt

Steps

  1. Fill two gallon pot with one gallon of water.
  2. Remove stones and dirt from dried beans, rinse and place in pot.
  3. Add garlic and cumin.
  4. Heat to a boil, then turn down to simmer.
  5. Brown ground beef in a skillet, drain oil and add meat to beans.
  6. Continue simmering beans until soft (approximately 3 hours).
  7. Add salt to own preference
  8. Serve in bowls.
  9. Enjoy!!
